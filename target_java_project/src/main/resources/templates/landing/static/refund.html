To get a refund for a consultation:

  1. Cancel the consultation in your personal cabinet no later than 24 hours before the start...

  2. In the email body, specify the following data:

    1. Payment amount for the consultation

    2. Card used for payment

    3. Passport series and number

    4. Reason for cancellation

    5. Contact information

  3. Refund is processed within 30 days from the application date.
